Trig Identities

Pythagorean & Quotient Identities
sin²θ + cos²θ = 1
Pythagorean Identity
Also: sin²θ = 1 − cos²θ and cos²θ = 1 − sin²θ
1 + tan²θ = sec²θ
Tangent-Secant Identity
Derived by dividing Pythagorean identity by cos²θ
1 + cot²θ = cosec²θ
Cotangent-Cosecant Identity
Derived by dividing Pythagorean identity by sin²θ
tan θ = sin θ / cos θ
Quotient Identity

Sine & Cosine Rules

Formulas
a/sinA = b/sinB = c/sinC
Sine Rule
Use when: two angles + one side, or two sides + non-included angle (watch for ambiguous case)
a² = b² + c² − 2bc cosA
Cosine Rule
Use when: two sides + included angle, or all three sides. Rearrange: cosA = (b²+c²−a²)/(2bc)
Area = ½ ab sinC
Area of Triangle
C = included angle between sides a and b
Area = ½ × base × height
Area (right-angle case)
Area = √[s(s−a)(s−b)(s−c)]
Heron's Formula
s = (a+b+c)/2 = semi-perimeter
